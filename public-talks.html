Public talks

Free Public Talks

Educational and public-interest talks on AI, work, meaning, existential management, leadership and the human side of technological change.

Conversations about staying human in the AI age

Meaning After Work offers selected public talks without a speaking fee for universities, schools, libraries, public institutions, non-profit organizations and educational events.

The talks explore how artificial intelligence changes not only jobs and skills, but also identity, meaning, responsibility, leadership, burnout, personal crisis and the way people remain present to one another under pressure.

These talks are offered as part of the public development of Meaning After Work and the Existential Human-Centred AI Forum. They are not commercial training products, consulting packages, therapy, diagnosis or clinical support.

Availability

Talks are offered free of charge when possible and subject to availability. Organizers may cover reasonable travel, accommodation or meal costs where needed. Online talks may also be possible.

For invitations, write to contact@meaningafterwork.com.

Important note

Educational, not clinical or commercial

Talks on burnout, personal crisis, existential management or meaning are educational and reflective. They are not therapy, coaching, diagnosis, mental health treatment, legal advice or workplace investigation.

Participants should not share confidential workplace information, sensitive personal data, medical information or identifiable third-party information during public sessions.

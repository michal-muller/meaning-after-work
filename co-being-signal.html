Co-being Signal

A short game about staying connected under intelligent noise.

Can you keep the signal alive?

Two people are trying to remain present to one another while deadlines, metrics, AI summaries, optimization demands and misunderstandings move between them.

Your task is not to click everything. Human signals need to be heard. Some system noise should pass. Some pressure must be named before it grows. Co-being is the fragile practice of staying with another person when everything pushes toward speed, function and defence.

How to play

  • Click human signals to increase presence and trust.
  • Click repair signals to restore the connection.
  • Be careful with system noise. Clicking it may reduce pressure briefly, but can weaken trust.
  • Do not let pressure and noise overwhelm the signal.

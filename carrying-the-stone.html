Reflective Experience / Prototype

Carrying the Stone

A visual reflective experience about work, AI and the burden of being human.

This is a reflective tool, not a diagnostic or therapeutic instrument. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

The stone is not only the work. It is the meaning we have attached to being useful.

How it works

You will move through five moments in which work, AI and human worth become entangled. Your choices do not produce a diagnosis. They shape a short reflection about how you carry pressure, agency, relationship and meaning.

The aim is not to win. The aim is to notice what happens to the stone.

The stone waits.

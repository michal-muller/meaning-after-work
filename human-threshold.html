Reflective Game / Prototype

The Human Threshold

A short game about staying human under pressure.

This is a reflective game, not a diagnostic, therapeutic or productivity assessment tool. Your personal best is stored only in your browser.

The challenge is not to maximize everything. The challenge is to remain human while pressure rises.

How it works

You will face a fast sequence of workplace and AI-related pressures. Each decision changes five dimensions: Meaning, Agency, Responsibility, Co-being and Pressure.

Your score rises when you preserve a living human balance under pressure. The game ends when time runs out, or when pressure and collapse become too strong.

The goal is not pure efficiency. The goal is to stay above the human threshold.

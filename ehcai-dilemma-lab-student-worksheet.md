## Purpose of the activity

In this simulation, you will work with a management dilemma involving artificial intelligence.

Your task is not to decide whether AI is good or bad.

Your task is to understand:

* what the AI system makes visible
* what the AI system makes invisible
* who remains responsible
* what a human-centred manager should do next

What is an existential contradiction?

An existential contradiction is not simply a problem to be solved. It is a human situation in which different demands pull us in incompatible directions.

We may feel pulled between efficiency and dependency, care and exhaustion, responsibility and survival, creativity and skill erosion, productivity and meaning, or adaptation and self-betrayal.

The purpose of this diary is not to remove contradiction immediately. It is to help you name it, understand its pressures and ask what responsible agency might look like within it.

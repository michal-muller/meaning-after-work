Contradiction Labyrinth

A reflective path through AI, work, pressure and human contradiction.

This is a reflective tool, not a diagnostic or therapeutic instrument. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

A contradiction is not always a problem to solve. Sometimes it is a passage to become more responsible.

How it works

You will enter five rooms. Each room presents a contradiction around AI, work, usefulness, responsibility and human relationship. There are no correct answers.

Your choices leave traces: Resignation, Shortcut, Revolt and Co-being. At the end, the Labyrinth returns a short reflection on how you tend to move through contradiction.

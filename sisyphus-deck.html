Reflective Experience / Prototype

Sisyphus Deck

A reflective card experience about AI, work and the burden of being human.

This is a reflective tool, not a diagnostic or therapeutic instrument. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

How it works

You will move through five short situations about AI, work, pressure and judgment. There are no correct answers. Each choice shifts a reflective path across four dimensions: Meaning, Agency, Responsibility and Co-being.

At the end, you will receive a brief reflection on how you tend to carry the stone.

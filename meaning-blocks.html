Reflective Experience / Prototype

Meaning Blocks

A reflective falling-block experience about AI, work, care, pressure and human meaning.

This is a reflective tool, not a diagnostic or therapeutic instrument. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

Life is not an empty grid. Every block you place changes the pattern of what remains human.

How it works

Blocks will appear one by one. Each block represents a force in contemporary work: AI, deadlines, care, responsibility, ambition, rest or reflection.

Place each block into the grid. Some combinations increase pressure. Others open space for meaning, agency or co-being.

There are no correct answers. The aim is to notice the pattern you are building.
